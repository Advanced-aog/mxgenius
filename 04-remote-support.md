---

## How Remote Support Works

### Instant Guidance at Your Fingertips

When a discrepancy arises, MXGenius immediately provides:

- **Step-by-step troubleshooting procedures** tailored to your specific aircraft and issue
- **Clear reset procedures** with visual guidance and reference to approved maintenance manuals
- **Real-time decision support** to help you determine if the issue can be resolved on-site
- **Interactive checklists** ensuring nothing is missed during troubleshooting

### Smart Escalation Protocol

If the issue cannot be resolved through self-guided troubleshooting, MXGenius automatically:

1. **Generates a detailed discrepancy report** containing all diagnostic data, troubleshooting steps attempted, and aircraft-specific information
2. **Routes the issue** to the appropriate maintenance provider or Advanced AOG network partner
3. **Provides status updates** so you know exactly when help is on the way
4. **Maintains full documentation** for regulatory compliance and trend analysis

---

## When Escalation Happens

MXGenius intelligently determines when an issue requires expert intervention:

- **Complex system failures** that exceed operator-level troubleshooting
- **Safety-critical discrepancies** requiring certified maintenance personnel
- **Recurring issues** that indicate deeper systemic problems
- **Time-sensitive situations** where delays could result in AOG

You remain in control—escalation can be initiated manually at any time, or MXGenius will recommend escalation based on diagnostic analysis.
