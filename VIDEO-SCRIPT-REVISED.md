# MXGenius Product Video Script
## Comprehensive 80-Second Overview Video

---

## Script Overview

**Total Duration:** 80 seconds  
**Target Audience:** Aircraft operators, MRO facilities, maintenance teams  
**Tone:** Professional, confident, solution-focused  
**Emotional Arc:** Problem (fear) → Relief (solution) → Confidence (results)  
**Format:** Narrated product walkthrough with UI demonstrations and B-roll

---

## Production Notes

### Audio Direction
- **Music Track:** Corporate/tech underscore, medium tempo (120-130 BPM)
  - 0-15s: Subtle, slightly tense (building problem awareness)
  - 15-75s: Confident, uplifting, modern (solution-focused)
  - 75-80s: Strong resolve, memorable finish
- **Voiceover:** Professional male or female, authoritative but approachable
  - Pace: Conversational, not rushed (approximately 140-160 words per minute)
  - Tone: Confident expert who understands operator pain points

### Visual Style
- **UI Shots:** Clean screen recordings, minimal cursor movement
- **B-Roll:** High-quality aircraft maintenance footage when specified
- **Transitions:** Smooth cuts, no flashy effects (maintain professional tone)
- **Color Grading:** Clean, slightly cool (tech-forward but aviation-appropriate)

### Caption Style
- **Font:** Sans-serif, bold, highly readable
- **Max Length:** 6 words per caption
- **Placement:** Lower third, centered
- **Animation:** Simple fade in/out (0.2s)

## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Color Grading
- Slightly cool tone overall (tech-forward)
- UI shots: Increase contrast slightly for screen readability
- B-roll: Natural aviation lighting, avoid over-saturation

### Audio Mixing
- **Voiceover:** -6dB to -3dB (primary element)
- **Music:** -18dB to -12dB (supportive, not distracting)
- **Sound Effects:** Minimal use (subtle UI click sounds at 0:22, 0:37, 0:47)

### Export Settings
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD minimum), 4K if source allows
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (matches most UI recordings)
- **Codec:** H.264, High Profile
- **Bitrate:** 10-15 Mbps (high quality for web)
- **Format:** MP4

### Platform-Specific Exports

#### YouTube/Website (Primary)
- 1920x1080, 30fps, 10Mbps
- Closed captions embedded (SRT file)

#### Instagram/Facebook
- 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1920 (vertical story format)
- Hard-coded captions (many viewers watch muted)
- 15s, 30s, and 60s cuts

#### LinkedIn
- 1920x1080, 30fps
- Hard-coded captions
- Professional tone emphasized
