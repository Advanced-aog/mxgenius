## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What platforms does MXGenius connect to?**  
A: We integrate with all major aviation parts listing platforms, OEM direct sources, and a network of verified distributors and brokers. Our coverage includes both new and used/surplus parts across all aircraft types.

**Q: How do you verify suppliers?**  
A: All suppliers in our network undergo verification including business licensing, insurance, regulatory compliance (FAA, EASA), and transaction history review. We also collect user ratings and feedback.

**Q: What's the typical transaction fee?**  
A: Transaction fees typically range from 2-4% depending on transaction size and complexity. The exact fee is disclosed before you commit to any purchase.

**Q: Can I still work with my existing vendors?**  
A: Absolutely. MXGenius complements your existing relationships. Use our platform to verify you're getting competitive pricing or to source hard-to-find parts.

**Q: What if I don't complete a purchase?**  
A: You pay nothing. Search, compare, and submit RFQs completely free. Fees only apply when you complete a transaction through our platform.

**Q: How does documentation work?**  
A: All required certifications (8130-3, EASA Form 1, traceability documents) are collected automatically as part of the transaction and stored in your account for easy retrieval and audit compliance.

**Q: Is there a minimum commitment?**  
A: No. Use MXGenius as much or as little as you need. No contracts, no minimums, no monthly fees.
