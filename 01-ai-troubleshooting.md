## Technical Details (In Plain Language)

### Intelligent Discrepancy Intake
Unlike basic ticketing systems, MXGenius understands aviation maintenance context. When you report a discrepancy:
- The AI recognizes aircraft-specific terminology (e.g., "EICAS message" vs "annunciator light")
- It adapts questions based on your aircraft type and systems
- Photo analysis identifies components and fault codes from images
- Video uploads allow you to show intermittent issues that are hard to describe

### Comprehensive Knowledge Base Integration
MXGenius doesn't just search—it *understands* your maintenance library:
- **Maintenance Manuals (MM):** Chapter and task card level indexing
- **Illustrated Parts Catalogs (IPC):** Visual part identification and cross-referencing
- **Service Bulletins (SB):** Automatic applicability checking for your tail numbers
- **Airworthiness Directives (AD):** Compliance tracking and related issue flagging
- **Wiring Diagrams:** Circuit tracing and connector identification
- **Troubleshooting Manuals (TSM):** Fault isolation procedures

### Automated Cross-Referencing
When troubleshooting an issue, the system simultaneously:
1. Checks if any open ADs relate to the reported symptoms
2. Identifies relevant service bulletins that may address the root cause
3. Flags recent fleet-wide patterns or recurring discrepancies
4. Suggests preventive actions based on manufacturer recommendations
5. Verifies required inspections or tests before return to service

### Machine Learning That Gets Smarter
Our AI improves through:
- **Resolution feedback:** Technicians confirm which solutions worked

- **Fleet analytics:** Learns from your specific aircraft usage and operating environment
- **Industry data:** Aggregated insights from anonymized maintenance events across operators
